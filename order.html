HOW TO ORDER

Instructions:

1. Go to the SHOP section to browse all available products.

2. Click ADD TO CART on the items you want to purchase.

3. When ready, click the shopping bag icon and proceed to CHECKOUT.

4. Enter your email address and shipping information.

5. Click CONTINUE TO SHIPPING.

6. Choose your payment method: MAYA or GCASH.

7. Click COMPLETE ORDER.

8. Take note of your order number.
